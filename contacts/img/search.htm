      Sven Magnus en Carlsen is a Norwegian chess grandmaster and the current World Chess Champion. A chess prodigy, Carlsen earned his grandmaster title at the age of 13 years and 148 days. Wikipedia
      Born: November 30, 1990 (age 26), Tnsberg, Norway
      World Champion: 2013present
      FIDE rating: 2837 (November 2017)
      Parents: Sigrun en, Henrik Albert Carlsen
      Siblings: Ingrid Carlsen, Signe Carlsen, Ellen en Carlsen
      Did you know: Magnus Carlsen ranks third in the world by months at FIDE number one (89). wikipedia.org
